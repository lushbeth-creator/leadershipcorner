Keep the Pipeline Flywheel Spinning
This isn't a report you fill out once and file away. It's one you keep working, with your Director and your VP, for as long as you're leading a team.
How It Works
Add to it every day. Some days that's one opportunity, some days it's four or five, some days it's taking one off because it's clearly not going to happen. Do this consistently and the report becomes something you value for yourself, not something handed down from leadership. It isn't about micromanaging. It's about staying aware of opportunity while it's happening, instead of noticing it after the fact.
Pair It With a Growth Conversation
Ask your Pilot where they want to be in two, three, or six months, and build the plan backward from there. A pipeline stays sharp when it's tied to where someone is actually trying to go.
