Beyond the Minimum
From Steady to Top Gun
A Tenured Pilot hitting their goals and minimum is doing fine. Fine isn't the ceiling. Use this list to find out whether they're actually plugged in, or just coasting on what already works.
Are they still checking in with L&D and leadership, or have those touchpoints quietly stopped?
Are they attending Learning Lift-Off and Flight School, or have they decided they're past needing it?
Do they know about the change from B2B Level 2 and Level 3?
Are they taking advantage of industry changes like Visa CEDP?
Are they aware of the Amex OptBlue annual minimum increases and what that means for their accounts?
Do they have a specific next milestone, or are they just repeating last quarter?
