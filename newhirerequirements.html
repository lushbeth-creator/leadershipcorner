New Hire Requirements
Before a Rookie Pilot Hits the Tracker
New Hire Tracker Agreement
A Rookie Pilot is not added to any monthly tab in the tracker until both boxes are checked: signed agreement and HR clear-to-start. Both, every time, no exceptions. If either one is still pending, hold off sending it through until it's confirmed.
2-Week Advance Notice
We're reinstating the 2-week advance notice requirement. Agreements need to be signed and new hires cleared to start by HR no less than 2 weeks before their class date. This is what gives HR the runway to complete clearance and gives Operations the runway to secure licensing, so we're not requesting or paying for access that ends up unused. A Leadership Note has been added to the tracker with more detail. Please read it and confirm in the acknowledgment section.
Curriculum & Learning Lift-Off Requirement
Every Rookie Pilot must complete all onboarding curriculum and assignments from In-Flight Training, and attend every Learning Lift-Off, within their first four months. This is a requirement, not a suggestion. Confirm progress at each check-in rather than assuming it's happening in the background.
