Pipeline Discipline
Pipeline means full, no placeholders. Every conversation should lead to a real next step. Follow up same day whenever possible, speed wins. Ask for the close earlier than feels comfortable. Handle objections live instead of getting stuck in email back-and-forth. Stay focused on decision makers only, "just checking in" isn't a next step.
